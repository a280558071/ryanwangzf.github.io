Research Interest

 I am a last-year M.S student in Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Shao-Lun Huang and Prof. Khalid M. Mosalam. I work on machine learning application in NLP and recommender systems. For this line of research, I also work closely with Prof. Ercan E. Kuruoglu and Dr. Yefeng Zheng . Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Uncertainty-aware machine learning and deep learning
  • Counterfactual learning and its application in recommender systems
  • Robust machine learning on noisy data
  • Graph convolutional networks
